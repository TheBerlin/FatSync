Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide our synchronization service, we collect the following information:

  • Account Info: Email and name provided via Google Authentication.
  • Health Data: Nutrition, calories, and weight logs fetched from your FatSecret account.
  • Notion Metadata: Database IDs and structure needed to map your sync.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used strictly for the purpose of moving information from FatSecret to Notion. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or health information to third parties.

3. Security

We use AES-256 encryption to protect your API tokens. We utilize official OAuth 2.0 flows, meaning we never see or store your passwords for FatSecret or Notion.
